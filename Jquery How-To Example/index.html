How To Walk Like a Person

Step 1

Start in a standing position

Step 2

Move your left or right leg in front of your body

Step 3

Shift your weight onto the leg in front of you

Step 4

Propel your torso forward toward the forward leg and bring your other leg to your forward leg.

Step 5

You have just taken your first step, repeat the whole process!
